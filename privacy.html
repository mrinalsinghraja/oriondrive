Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

🔒 Short version: Orion Drive does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data or files. We connect directly to Google's API using your own account credentials. Your files never pass through our servers — because we don't have any.

1. Who We Are

Orion Drive is a macOS application developed by MSRX. The app is available on the Mac App Store and allows users to manage files across Google Drive and local Mac storage from a single interface.

Contact: mrinalsinghraja@gmail.com

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Orion Drive collects no personal data whatsoever. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or profile information
  • The names, contents, or metadata of your files
  • Your Google Drive file structure or folder names
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or diagnostic data
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • Any telemetry or behavioral tracking data

3. How Google Account Access Works

To access your Google Drive files, Orion Drive uses Google OAuth 2.0 — the same industry-standard authentication used by all legitimate Google integrations.

  • You log in directly through Google's official sign-in page
  • Google issues an access token that allows Orion Drive to communicate with Google Drive on your behalf
  • This token is stored only on your Mac in the system Keychain — encrypted and inaccessible to other apps
  • We never see your Google password
  • You can revoke access at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions

4. Google API Scopes Used

Orion Drive requests the following Google API permissions:

  • Google Drive (drive) — to list, browse, upload, download, rename, move and delete your Drive files
  • Email address (userinfo.email) — to display your email in the app sidebar
  • Basic profile (userinfo.profile) — to display your name and storage quota

These permissions are used exclusively to provide the app's file management features. No data from these scopes is transmitted to MSRX or any third party.

5. Local File Access

When you grant Orion Drive access to local folders (Desktop, Downloads, Documents, iCloud Drive), the app reads and displays those files only within the app on your device. No local file data is transmitted anywhere.

Local folder access is granted via macOS's standard security-scoped file access system. You can revoke access at any time in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Files and Folders.

6. Data Storage

Orion Drive stores the following data only on your Mac:

  • OAuth tokens — stored in the macOS Keychain, encrypted by the operating system
  • App preferences — stored in UserDefaults (view mode, sort order, etc.)
  • Security-scoped bookmarks — stored in UserDefaults to remember which local folders you've granted access to
  • Preview cache — temporary files in the system temp directory, automatically cleared

None of this data is shared with MSRX or uploaded anywhere.

7. Third-Party Services

Orion Drive communicates exclusively with Google's official APIs (googleapis.com). We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data processing services.

8. Children's Privacy

Orion Drive is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
